Solo Leveling

Genre: Action, Fantasy, Adventure

Author: Chugong

Synopsis: In a world where hunters battle monsters, the weakest hunter, Sung Jin-Woo, finds himself in a deadly dungeon. After a near-fatal encounter, he gains a mysterious ability that allows him to "level up" and become the strongest hunter. Follow his journey as he rises from the lowest rank to the pinnacle of power, uncovering secrets and facing formidable foes along the way.
